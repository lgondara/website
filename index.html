About

I am a machine learning researcher, currently working as a scientist and AI team lead at Provincial Health Services Authority. I also hold an Adjunct Professor position at University of British Columbia. My current work focuses on language models (large and small) and their applications in various domains. My broader research interests include general machine learning, differential privacy, and multimodal machine learning.

Research Interests

Language Models

Large and small language models with applications across various domains, particularly in resource-constrained environments. Agentic methods and model grounding.

Privacy-Preserving ML

Differential privacy and privacy-preserving machine learning techniques for sensitive data applications.

Applied Machine Learning

Applying machine learning to solve complex real-world problems using approaches for text, vision, tabular, and multimodal data. Focus on translating research into practical solutions.
